Features of Medicine Shop/Pharmacy Management Software

Customers

You can add unlimited customers name and their details like address, mobile number, email address etc.

Medicine

You can add unlimited medicine or item here with stock information, supplier name, inventory info, barcode number etc.

Suppliers

You can save your supplier(Pharmaceuticals company) information here with their contact details.

Receiving

If client back your medicine then this option will help you to receive the product that will hit your inventory

Sales

This option help your sales person to sale your medicine from stock & receive the payment by cash or card

Employees

You can add unlimited stuff with individual access level. Employee can use this software according to their access level.

Store Configuration

You can set or edit your medicine shop information, address, phone number here.

Barcode

Barcode integrated purchase and sales system is available here.
